Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our female trainers and personal training services in Dubai

What qualifications do your female trainers have?

Our female trainers hold internationally recognized certifications including NASM, ACE, ISSA, and specialized women's fitness certifications. They also have expertise in prenatal/postnatal fitness, women's health, and nutrition. All trainers have a minimum of 5 years of experience and undergo continuous education to stay updated with the latest fitness trends and women's health research.

How do I choose the right female trainer for my goals?

We offer a free consultation where you can discuss your fitness goals, experience level, and preferences. Our female trainers specialize in different areas such as weight loss, toning, prenatal/postnatal fitness, women's health, and strength training. We'll match you with a trainer whose expertise aligns with your specific goals and training style preferences.

What is the cost of personal training with female trainers?

Personal training sessions with our female trainers start from AED 200 per session. We offer various packages including single sessions, monthly packages, and long-term commitments with discounted rates. All packages include personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking. Contact us for detailed pricing and package options.

Do female trainers work with male clients?

Yes, our female trainers work with clients of all genders. We believe in providing professional, respectful training environments where all clients feel comfortable and supported. Many of our female trainers have extensive experience working with male clients and understand the specific needs and goals that may differ from female clients.

What training styles do female trainers specialize in?

Our female trainers specialize in various training styles including strength training, functional fitness, yoga, Pilates, HIIT, women's health fitness, prenatal/postnatal training, and rehabilitation. Each trainer has unique expertise areas, and we'll match you with someone whose specialization aligns with your goals and preferred training style.

How often should I train with a female trainer?

The frequency depends on your goals, fitness level, and schedule. For beginners, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. Intermediate clients typically do 2 sessions per week, while advanced clients might do 1-2 sessions per week with additional independent workouts. Your trainer will create a personalized plan that fits your lifestyle and goals.
