Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and training programs

What services do you offer?

We offer personal training, online coaching, nutrition plans, and group classes tailored to your fitness goals in Abu Dhabi. Our certified trainers specialize in weight loss, muscle gain, and rehabilitation.

How do I get started?

Begin with a free consultation where we assess your fitness level and discuss goals. We'll then create a personalized plan and schedule your first session at our Abu Dhabi facility or online.

What are your training rates?

Our Abu Dhabi personal training rates start from AED 250 per session, with discounts available for packages. Contact us for exact pricing based on your preferred program and trainer.

Do you offer online training?

Yes, we provide comprehensive online training with custom workouts, video check-ins, and nutrition guidance. Perfect for Abu Dhabi residents who prefer home workouts.

What qualifications do your trainers have?

All trainers hold international certifications (NASM, ACE, ISSA) and specialize in areas like weight loss, bodybuilding, and injury rehabilitation. We only hire the best for our Abu Dhabi clients.

Can I train with a specific trainer?

Absolutely! We'll match you with the perfect Abu Dhabi trainer based on your goals and personality during your free consultation.

What if I have an injury or health condition?

Our Abu Dhabi trainers specialize in rehabilitation and will create a safe, customized program in coordination with your healthcare provider.

Do you offer nutrition guidance?

Yes, we provide personalized nutrition plans using locally available foods in Abu Dhabi, along with restaurant guides and supplement advice.
