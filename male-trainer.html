Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our male trainers and personal training services in Dubai

What qualifications do your male trainers have?

Our male trainers hold internationally recognized certifications including NASM, ACE, ISSA, and CrossFit Level 1. They also have specialized certifications in nutrition, injury prevention, and functional training. All trainers have a minimum of 5 years of experience and undergo continuous education to stay updated with the latest fitness trends and techniques.

How do I choose the right male trainer for my goals?

We offer a free consultation where you can discuss your fitness goals, experience level, and preferences. Our trainers specialize in different areas such as weight loss, muscle building, sports performance, and rehabilitation. We'll match you with a trainer whose expertise aligns with your specific goals and training style preferences.

What is the cost of personal training with male trainers?

Personal training sessions with our male trainers start from AED 200 per session. We offer various packages including single sessions, monthly packages, and long-term commitments with discounted rates. All packages include personalized workout plans, nutrition guidance, and progress tracking. Contact us for detailed pricing and package options.

Do male trainers work with female clients?

Yes, our male trainers work with clients of all genders. We believe in providing professional, respectful training environments where all clients feel comfortable and supported. Many of our male trainers have extensive experience working with female clients and understand the specific needs and goals that may differ from male clients.

What training styles do male trainers specialize in?

Our male trainers specialize in various training styles including strength training, functional fitness, CrossFit, HIIT, bodybuilding, sports-specific training, and rehabilitation. Each trainer has unique expertise areas, and we'll match you with someone whose specialization aligns with your goals and preferred training style.

How often should I train with a male trainer?

The frequency depends on your goals, fitness level, and schedule. For beginners, we recommend 2-3 sessions per week. Intermediate clients typically do 2 sessions per week, while advanced clients might do 1-2 sessions per week with additional independent workouts. Your trainer will create a personalized plan that fits your lifestyle and goals.
